# Concept Art Direction

## Gameplay fantasy

The player commands a growing company of specialist dwarves defending an ancient underground stronghold. Each run alternates between tactical preparation, continuous enemy waves, and meaningful upgrades that enable the company to survive deeper sections of the kingdom.

## What the mockup communicates

### Battlefield

The environment combines a monumental hall, narrow passage, staircase, raised positions, and chokepoints. These features are intended to make placement and formation choices as important as raw damage.

### Dwarven company

The initial visual roster explores six complementary roles:

| Role | Battlefield identity | Example synergy |
|---|---|---|
| Iron Warden | Blocks lanes and protects allies | Extends Rune-smith wards |
| Berserker | High-risk area damage | Gains tempo from Brewer buffs |
| Ranger | Long-range priority targeting | Fires bolts ignited by the Engineer |
| Rune-smith | Wards, control, and magical support | Reinforces defensive formations |
| Engineer | Turrets, traps, and elemental effects | Enables fire-based combinations |
| Brewer | Healing and temporary combat buffs | Sustains aggressive front-line units |

### Enemy pressure

Enemy waves mix numerous light creatures, armored attackers, disruptive monsters, and large threats. The distant flame-and-shadow creature represents the kind of boss encounter that should force the player to rethink a previously successful formation.

### Progression

The interface suggests two resources, unit levels, branching forge upgrades, cooldowns, fortress durability, and wave objectives. These are exploratory elements rather than finalized systems.

## Visual principles

- Crisp, readable pixel art with a consistent pixel scale.
- Warm torchlight against cold stone and deep subterranean shadows.
- Strong silhouettes and color-coded effects for quick combat readability.
- Carved stone and forged metal UI elements that reinforce the setting.
- Dense atmosphere without obscuring tactical information.

## Status

This artwork is a visual target, not a final gameplay specification. Mechanics, scope, and technical constraints will be defined during planning.
